Unrivaled Diamond Standards

Our approach to the diamond selection process is one of utmost reverence and meticulous care. We ensure that each diamond inspected is issued a GIA diamond certification, distinguished by an engraved identification number and accompanying certificate.

THE BIRKS BELL BOX

For over 100 years, our signature Bell Box has accompanied Birks engagement rings. A cherished symbol of our heritage, this precious keepsake is reminiscent of wedding bells and evokes the joy and anticipation of starting a new chapter. We are honoured that our iconic silver Bell Box holds a special place in the hearts of couples embarking on their journey together.
